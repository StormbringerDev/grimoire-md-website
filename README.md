# grimoire.md

Computing is probably as close to real magic as we're going to get in the real world. However, to truly understand this wonder, one must acquire the means to harness this magic. That's why I've developed a public spellbook in the form of **grimoire.md** for understanding and manipulating the 1s and 0s that make up our entire modern digital infrastructure.
